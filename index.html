How it works

The workflow that
works for you.

From intelligence ingestion to client-ready deliverable — every step of the process, running at the pace your team needs.

Step 01
Intelligence ingested

368 curated sources across policy, parliamentary proceedings, enforcement actions, industry analysis, and strategic commentary — arriving via sector-tagged inboxes, parsed and organised 24 hours a day.

Step 02
Fieldwork frames the analysis

Your firm's sector expertise, methodology, and advisory perspective — encoded in Fieldwork — shapes how every piece of intelligence is interpreted. What your firm considers material. In your firm's language.

Step 03
ICP relevance scored

Every development is scored against every Ideal Client Profile. The same news reads differently for a fintech client versus a media company. Fieldwork produces the right insight for the right client, precisely.

Step 04
Deliverables generated

Flash Insights, Intelligence Notes, Company Briefings — written in your firm's voice, tailored to the specific client, distributed via email, Teams, Slack, or LinkedIn. Your name on it. Your team's time back.

What makes Fieldwork different

Structural advantages.
Not feature lists.

Your firm's perspective, encoded

Two firms using Fieldwork on the same development will get two different outputs. Fieldwork encodes your sector expertise, methodology, and advisory framing — and applies it to every analysis, permanently.

Multi-client by design

Every development is scored against every ICP simultaneously. A firm with thirty clients gets thirty relevance-filtered views of the same intelligence — at the moment it matters. This is the architecture no competitor has attempted.

The output is the deliverable

Fieldwork doesn't give you intelligence to interpret — it gives you finished briefings, written in your firm's voice, with your firm's name on them.

368 curated sources

A maintained library of verified sources spanning policy, parliamentary proceedings, enforcement actions, industry analysis, and strategic commentary — across 80 markets. Built and maintained by Aysgarth.

Events intelligence

Upcoming conferences, parliamentary hearings, and key milestones are curated and scored as they're published. Know who is speaking, match them against your client roster, and have a personalised outreach message drafted — all before the agenda goes public.
